About Raj

200 hours

Based in
Den Haag, NL

Structure brought me to the mat.
Curiosity kept me here.

I’m Raj Sherjeet Singh—also known by my secular name, Slavik. My background is in software engineering, a world of systems, problem-solving, and long hours at a desk.

Over five years, yoga became a counterbalance: a way to feel more present, capable, and at home in my body. In 2026 I completed a 200-hour Yoga Teacher Training at Arhanta Yoga Netherlands. I’m now an aspiring teacher, sharing this practice with genuine care and enthusiasm.
